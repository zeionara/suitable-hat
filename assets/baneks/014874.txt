A friend of Mr and Mrs Dunn was going to London. Mr Dunn asked him to check on his son Neally that lives in London, cause he was worried about him. He didn't wrote to him in a long time.Mr Dunn's friend agreed and asked where does he live. Mr Dunn said that the address is London WC1.So when he arrived in London, he was going through the airport and he saw the door with the "WC" on it. He went in and there were another couple of doors.He knocked on the first one and said:- Excuse me, are you Neally Dunn?The voice from the other side answered:- Yes, I'm done, but I can't find any paper.- It's not an excuse for not writing to your parents!
